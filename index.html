About Me

I was born here in the state of Idaho, in a then small farming community about 90 east of here. I am retired, but not tired of life yet. I served in the US Air Force and am still a 50-year member of it's Auxiliary called the Civil Air Patrol. I am a private pilot, and still love to get into the air where I am an aircrew member during training and actual missions for Disasters and Search and Rescue.

When I am not crewing, I serve as duty officer and commander in the Incident Command areas. When not involved here, or at church, I love to continue learning about anything involving computers, medical, or geologic.

Idaho is known for agriculture, mostly from 'Famous Idaho Potatos.' Though you will see lots of sugar beets also. We still have some open space here including the "Frank Church Wilderness" where cars, boats, or other motorized travel is forbidden.
