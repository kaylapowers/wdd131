About Me

Kayla Powers:

I was born here in the state of Idaho, in a then small farming community about 90 east of here. I am retired, but not tired of life yet. I served in the US Air Force and am still a 50-year member of it's Auxiliary called the Civil Air Patrol. I am a private pilot, and still love to get into the air where I am an aircrew member during training and actual missions for Disasters and Search and Rescue. When I am not crewing, I serve as duty officer and commander in the Incident Command areas.
