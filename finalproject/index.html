HOME PAGE

We are a full-time college and life preparatory academy that develops young leaders who want to focus on learning in an environment where prayer and worship are not only encouraged but scheduled into the learning day. Recruit staff, volunteers, and parents who will enroll their children in our school. Allow students to get excited about our program, and voluntarily commit to our more rigorous standards.

What Is A Vanguard?

The group that takes the lead in battles of all types. Our young vanguards are taught to be self-disciplined and prepared!

Vanguards are cadets up front in the action.
